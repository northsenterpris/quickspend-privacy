Analytics we collect

To understand how the App is used and to improve it, we use Google Firebase Analytics. This collects limited, non-identifying usage information, such as:

  • Which screens and features are used, and general app activity
  • Device type, operating system version, and app version
  • Approximate region (derived from IP address, not precise location)
  • A randomly generated analytics identifier (app instance ID)

This analytics data does not include your name, email, or any of the spending entries you record. It is processed by Google LLC, which may process data outside the EU/EEA under appropriate safeguards such as the EU Standard Contractual Clauses. See Google’s privacy information at policies.google.com/privacy.
